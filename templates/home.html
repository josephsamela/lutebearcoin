About

Welcome to the Lute Bear Coin Network!
The most, uh trustworthy(?) cryptocurreny network run by bears who play instruments.

How Does It Work?

When a transaction request is submitted, the bears look at it. If they approve, the transaction is added to the blockchain. Bears can't read.

Is It Decentralized?

Yes! We bought the bears a photocopy machine so they each have a copy of the ledger. This process is infallible and cannot be improved.

How Fast Are Transactions?

Honestly, it's pretty slow. Bears hibernate during the winter and spend the summer months eating salmon, berries and whatever people leave in dumpsters. But, they get to it eventually.

Does It Support NFTs?

Yes! Many great artworks are published and traded on the Lute Bear Coin network. I mean, most of the artwork is pretty bad. But don't tell the bears. It makes them feel sad.
