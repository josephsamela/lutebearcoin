How does it work?

Uh, well when a transaction request is submitted, one of the bears looks at it. If they approve the transaction is added to the blockchain. Bears can't read.

How do I submit a transaction?

Tell one of the bears and they'll probably figure it out. It's usually easiest to message them on Discord.

Is the blockchain decentralized?

Yes! We bought the bears a photocopy machine so they each have a copy.

How long do transactions take?

Honestly, it's pretty slow. Bears hibernate during the winter and spend the summer months eating salmon, bearries and whatever people leave in dumpsters. But, they usually get to it eventually.
